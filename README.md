Simple Image Viewer
===================

A fast, clean, fullscreen image viewer for Android designed primarily to stay out of your way and just display the image. Has support for animated images including GIF and WebP.

Usage
-----

### Viewing Images

- **Open Image**: Tap the screen when no image is loaded, or use the "Open Image" option in the radial menu.
- **Zoom/Pan**: Use standard pinch-to-zoom and drag gestures.
- **Quick Zoom**: Double-tap to instantly zoom in or out.

### Radial Menu

Hold two fingers down for a moment to reveal the stealth radial menu. While holding, select an option by sliding your fingers and target using the midpoint between them, then release to activate your selection.
